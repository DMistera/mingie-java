Welcome to the world of alchemy!
I will personally assist you in your journey to become master alchemist!
Combine elements to create new elements!
You can only combine elements you own.

**Available commands:**
**!me** - View your unlocked elements.
**!combine/!c [element] + [element]** - Combine elements
**!tip** - If you're stuck, I will give you a small tip!
**!suggest [element] [imageURL]** - If an element is missing image or you have a better image for a given element you can suggest your image to the game creator. Your suggestion will be considered shorty after.
